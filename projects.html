Air-Tight

Status - complete

Air-Tight is a survival game based on continuous movement. You will see yourselves in the act of trying to cross a deserted wasteland in the lack of oxygen. Your only companion: a giant animal that will transport all your belongings and oxygen stocks. Finding resources and upgrading the mount will give you more chances of survival.

GD Codex Algorithm

Status - ongoing

In video game development, it's getting tougher to find and research algorithms. The term "artificial intelligence" is overused and makes research of algorithms and automation techniques time-consuming. Often all due to the prevailing presence of machine learning algorithms.
The idea is to create a digital library that gathers AI applications, systems, and tools used in the game development community. This would be a starting point for game developers to understand the complexities of AI systems and their potential use in games.
Game designers and the game industry will gain from a structured database of information, documenting the pros and cons of systems and identifying areas that need more research. Codex Algorithm evolves with AI and its impact on game development. The virtual library builds a community and resource archive to track AI advancements and aid design decisions. Often, the best solution isn’t the most elaborate but the one that looks the smartest in the simplest way.

Pirate Pigeons

Status - ongoing

Pirate Pigeons - Drowning is an Option is a chaotic party game where it is all about working together. Players step into the role of a flightless and silly pigeon and embark on the most epic journey of her life. Only through solid teamwork the shabby raft will stick together and the crew is prepared to fight off all odds on their way into the big unknown
